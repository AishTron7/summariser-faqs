FAQs (for AI Review Summary Extension)



Q1. While installing the extension, I’m getting a message: “This extension is not trusted by Enhanced Safe Browsing.”
Ans: For new chrome developers, it generally takes a few months to become trusted. Please click on ‘continue to install’.

Q2. I get the summary of all reviews for products having 6-7 pages of reviews, but for products that have greater than ten pages of reviews, I only get the summary of first ten pages of reviews.
Ans: The current word capacity of the AI model used (GPT-3.5-turbo) is around 3000 words (4k tokens). We have found that 10 pages of reviews is the sweet spot for most products i.e. summarising more than 10 pages might exceed the 3000 word limit of OpenAI’s model. Moreover, we feel that a summary of ten pages gives a good enough idea of the product’s performance to the customer. We may increase this limit of ten pages as we get access to GPT 4 API which has a larger capacity.

Q3. I’m getting a message “reviews for this product might be too long for GPT's current capacity”
Ans: For some products, customers tend to give lengthy reviews, in such cases, ten pages of reviews can exceed the 3000 word limit set by OpenAI. Please use the extension for a different product for now, while we strive to mitigate this issue.

Q4. I’m getting a message: “A summary is not required due to lack of reviews” even when reviews are there
Ans: Make sure you are on the correct page. You do not need to click on ‘see all reviews’. Just open the product page on flipkart, open the extension, and click the ‘generate summary’ button.
